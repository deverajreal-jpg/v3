Build Start Date (Column M)

Date you started building the account.

N

Build Completed Date (Column N)

Date the account was finished building.

O

Posted For Sale Date (Column O)

Date you listed the account for sale. Used to calculate average sell time.

!

Update Apps Script doGet()

In your Apps Script, find the accounts push() inside doGet() and add these fields after roi: r[9]:

soldPlatform: r[10] || '',
currencyPaid: r[11] || '',
buildStart: safeDate(r[12]),
buildEnd: safeDate(r[13]),
postedDate: safeDate(r[14]),

Stats are pulled live from OSRS Hiscores via your Apps Script proxy — no manual tracking needed. The RSN in Column A must match the exact in-game username. Accounts not yet on hiscores (very new accounts) will show "Not on hiscores" until they have enough stats.
